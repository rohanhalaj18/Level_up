Mock Placement Training

A complete simulated placement experience designed to get you job-ready. We replicate the entire recruitment process to give you the confidence and skills to succeed.

See the Process

How It Works

1. Aptitude & Technical Test

Begin with a timed online test that covers quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, verbal ability, and core technical subjects, just like in a real placement drive.

2. Coding Round

Showcase your coding proficiency in a dedicated round with challenging problems designed to test your algorithmic and data structure knowledge.

3. Mock AI Interview

Experience a realistic, AI-powered interview that assesses your technical knowledge and communication skills, providing instant, unbiased feedback.

4. Comprehensive Feedback

Receive a detailed performance report with scores, a breakdown of strengths and weaknesses, and actionable advice for improvement.

5. HR Interview

Face a behavioral interview focused on assessing your communication skills, personality, and cultural fit for a corporate environment.

WARNINGS!!

1. Camera Position & Face Alignment

  • Sit in the center of the camera frame.
  • Your eyes must be clearly visible and looking toward the screen.
  • Ensure your full face is visible with proper lighting.

2. One Person Only (Strict Rule)

  • Only one person should appear in the camera view.
  • If the system detects more than one person, an alert will be shown.

3. Facial Expression & Attitude

  • Maintain a friendly smile and a positive attitude.
  • Avoid looking away repeatedly or showing disinterest.

4. Background Requirements

  • Use a clean and distraction-free background.
  • Avoid movement behind you or loud noises.

7. No External Help

  • Do not take help from other people, mobile phones, or books.
  • AI can detect eye movement if you read from the screen.

8. Body Language

  • Sit straight with a professional posture.
  • Avoid unnecessary movements or leaving the frame.

10. Honesty & Ethics

  • Do not use AI tools, scripts, or pre-written answers.
  • The system can detect unnatural response patterns.

11. Environment Rules

  • Choose a quiet space and silence your mobile phone.
  • Ensure no one interrupts during the session.

12. Time Management

  • Join the interview on time.
  • Use the limited time for each question wisely.
